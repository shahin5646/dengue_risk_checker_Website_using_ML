About DengueCheck

An AI-powered dengue risk assessment system designed to provide early detection, personalized risk scores, and actionable health recommendations for Dhaka residents.

Project Overview

Mission

DengueCheck aims to democratize dengue risk assessment through accessible AI technology. By combining machine learning with epidemiological data, we empower individuals and healthcare providers with early detection capabilities and data-driven insights.

Saving lives through early awareness and prevention.

How It Works

  • Analyzes demographic and geographic factors
  • Uses advanced ML models (XGBoost + Logistic Regression)
  • Provides personalized risk probability scores
  • Identifies top risk factors for your profile
  • Offers prevention guidance and recommendations

Key Features

AI-Powered Prediction

Utilizes machine learning models (XGBoost & Logistic Regression) trained on real dengue data from Dhaka region to provide accurate risk assessments.

Geographic Analysis

Identifies dengue hotspots and assesses area-specific risk factors to provide location-aware health recommendations.

Risk Stratification

Categorizes users into risk levels (Low, Moderate, High, Very High) with personalized prevention guidance for each level.

Factor Analysis

Explains which demographic and environmental factors most influence dengue risk, helping users understand their susceptibility.

Project Goals & Impact

Goal 1: Early Detection

Provide individuals with early dengue risk assessment capabilities to enable timely medical consultation and laboratory testing.

  • Binary classification (Dengue positive/negative)
  • Probability scoring (0-100%)
  • Risk stratification levels
  • Actionable recommendations

Goal 2: Risk Understanding

Identify and explain key dengue risk factors to support public health initiatives and awareness campaigns.

  • Feature importance ranking
  • Geographic hotspot identification
  • Demographic risk patterns
  • Healthcare provider insights
